Scrambled Eggs with Bacon

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • milk
  • salt and pepper
  • butter
  • and of course bacon

Steps

  1. Cook 2 pieces of bacon
  2. Crack eggs in a mixing bowl and add in the milk, salt, pepper and cooked bacon.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium high heat.
  4. Once the egg mixture seems to have set, break it up with a spatula to scramble and flip.
  5. Continue cooking and scrambling until eggs are fully cooked and no longer runny. Serve immediately and enjoy!
