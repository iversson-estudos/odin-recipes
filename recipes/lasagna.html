Lasagna

Lasagna with bechamel, marinara, sausage and beef is much better than a classic lasagna This recipe makes a fantastic Christmas dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Fresh Basil
  • Hot Italian Sausage

Steps

  1. Start by prepping all of your ingredients and making the three sauces needed for the lasagna.
  2. Then, place the dry noodles in a baking dish and cover with hot water to soften them.
  3. Spread a thin layer of bolognese on the bottom. Make a single layer of noodles, then add more meat sauce.
  4. Next, spread some of the bechamel over the top.
  5. Then, add a couple handfuls of mozzarella and parmesan, making sure to sprinkle it to the edges.
  6. Add another layer of noodles on top of the cheese.
  7. Continue layering until you have used up all of the ingredients.
  8. Bake until warmed all the way through.
  9. Let the lasagna rest so it can firm up before slicing.
